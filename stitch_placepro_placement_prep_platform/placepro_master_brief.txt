# PlacePro Master Brief

## Product Vision
A placement-prep platform for Indian college students blending social networking, LMS features, and gamification.

## Audience
Final-year and pre-final engineering/CS students in India.

## Tone & Style
Energetic, credible, achievement-driven. Clean SaaS UI, rounded cards, confident indigo primary, amber gamification pops.

## Core Navigation
- **Marketing:** Landing, Login, Signup
- **Onboarding:** 4-step wizard
- **App Shell (Student):** Dashboard, Feed, Interview Hub, Quizzes, Arena, Jobs, Roadmap, Resume, Leaderboard, Profile, Rooms
- **Admin Shell:** Admin Dashboard, Users, Teachers, Analytics, Content Management

## Visual Constraints
- 12-16px border radius
- Soft shadows
- No heavy gradients
- Outline iconography
- Persistent sidebars for authenticated states
